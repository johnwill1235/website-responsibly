Privacy Policy for Responsibly

Effective Date: March 30 2025

Age Restriction Notice

Responsibly is intended for users 21 years of age or older. This application is designed to track alcohol consumption for adults of legal drinking age in the United States (21+) and other applicable jurisdictions. If you are under the minimum legal drinking age in your jurisdiction, you are not permitted to use this app.

Information We Collect

Responsibly is designed with your privacy in mind:

  1. Local Data Storage: All of your drink tracking data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal drink information on remote servers.
  2. Age Verification: Your date of birth is collected solely for the purpose of verifying that you meet the minimum age requirement. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  3. Analytics: We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app's functionality. This may include information about:
    • App launches and screen views
    • Feature usage
    • Performance metrics
    • Device type and OS version
    This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

  • Age Verification: To ensure you meet the legal drinking age requirements
  • Local Functionality: To provide drink tracking and statistical analysis
  • Product Improvement: To identify and fix bugs, improve features, and enhance user experience

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information stored within the app. Since your data is stored locally on your device, maintaining the security of your device (through regular updates, secure passcodes, etc.) is the best way to keep your information secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

Your Choices

You can reset or delete all of your app data at any time through the app's settings menu or by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Age Verification

Responsibly implements age verification at the first launch of the application. Users must verify they are at least 21 years of age before using the app. This verification is stored locally and will not need to be repeated unless the app is reinstalled.
